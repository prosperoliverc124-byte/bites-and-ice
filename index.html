About Bites & Ice

A different treat from the usual.

At Bites & Ice, we serve refreshing drinks, sweet treats and snack-time favorites in a cozy, colorful space designed for cravings and good vibes. From bubble tea and popsicles to mini pancakes, pastries and more, there’s always something to enjoy.

Whether you’re stopping by for a quick dessert, hanging out with friends, or placing an order through WhatsApp, we’re here to make every visit feel fun, fresh and satisfying.
